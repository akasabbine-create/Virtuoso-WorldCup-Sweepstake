📜 Rules, Bonuses & Prizes

Basic Match Points

  • Win: 3 points
  • Draw: 1 point
  • Loss: 0 points

Each player earns points from all three of their assigned teams. For example, if two of your teams win, you receive 6 points.

Games Played

  • A game is counted once one of your teams has completed a match.
  • Only completed matches are included in the leaderboard.
  • Upcoming fixtures do not affect points or games played.

Leaderboard Ranking

  • Players are ranked by total points.
  • Total points are match points plus awarded bonus points.
  • If players are level on points, games played is used as the next sorter.
  • Movement arrows only show when a player’s score or games played has changed.

Current Badges

  • 🥄 Wooden Spoon: Current owner of the single worst-performing team.
  • 🥾 Golden Boot Race: Player who owns the team of the current top scorer.
  • ⚽ Most Goals Nation: Player who owns the current highest-scoring nation.
  • ⚡ Fastest Goal: Player who owns the team of the current fastest goal scorer.

Badges show the current race leaders only. They do not mean final bonus points or prizes have been awarded.

Knockout & Progression Bonuses

  • Reach Round of 32: +5 points
  • Reach Round of 16: +5 points
  • Reach Quarter-finals: +5 points
  • Reach Semi-finals: +10 points
  • Reach Final: +10 points
  • Tournament Winner: +15 points

Progression bonuses are awarded to the player who owns the team reaching each stage.

Performance Bonuses

  • Golden Boot: +5 points to the player who owns the team of the top scorer.
  • Most goals by nation: +5 points to the player who owns that nation.
  • Knockout clean sheet: +2 points per clean sheet in the knockout stage only.

Golden Boot and most-goals-by-nation bonuses are tracked during the tournament and awarded at the end.

Wooden Spoon

  • The wooden spoon is awarded to the player who owns the single worst-performing team.
  • It is not based on the player’s total score.
  • Worst team is calculated by lowest team points.
  • If tied, the team with more games played is considered worse.
  • If still tied, worst goal difference is used.
  • If still tied, fewest goals scored is used.

Wooden Spoon does not affect leaderboard points. It is a £5 prize awarded at the end of the tournament.

Prizes

  • Highest points total: £40
  • Runner-up: £20
  • Third place: £10
  • Fastest goal: £5 prize only, no leaderboard points
  • Wooden Spoon: £5 prize only, no leaderboard points

Prize awards are separate from leaderboard scoring unless explicitly listed as bonus points.

Automatic Updates

  • Results are pulled automatically from ESPN’s public scoreboard.
  • The leaderboard updates automatically through GitHub Actions.
  • The page refreshes from generated data files hosted on GitHub Pages.
